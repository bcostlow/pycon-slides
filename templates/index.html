PyCon Slide Deck Uploader

FAQ

Why are slides so important this year?
We will have real-time closed captioning for all the talks this year, and uploading your slide deck ahead of time will help the caption writer pre-populate their dictionary with any obscure terms or abbreviations you may use. Even an incomplete slide deck is useful!

What will happen to my slides?
Your slides will be uploaded to SpeakerDeck by a volunteer, and will be used by the real-time closed caption writer to help pre-populate their dictionary with any obscure terms you may be using.

Can I upload multiple times?
Yep, no problem! Upload early, upload often!

What format should I use?
PDF is best. Otherwise, whatever your source format is. If you're using HTML, zip the .html file and all required assets and upload the zip file. If your talk includes code or other examples, zip those and upload the zip file.

What if I made a mistake?
Don't worry about it. Just fix the mistake and upload again.
